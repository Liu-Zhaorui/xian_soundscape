# Circular Mound Altar Soundscape

## Spatial character

Circular mound sites combine open terraces with stepped rings. Wind and sightlines stay unobstructed, so the soundscape often feels thin and distant—clear steps nearby, urban hum far away.

## Listening cues

- **Wind on the mound**: Concentric paths refract airflow; what you hear changes as you move.
- **Ritual imagination**: Bells and drums need not sound in reality to feel present on an empty terrace.
- **Day and night**: Traffic masks detail by day; at dusk insects and quieter reflections emerge.

## Visiting tip

Walk slowly and notice how footfalls change between packed earth and stone; compare empty-field reverberation with any recorded or live gathering on the terrace.
